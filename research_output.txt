Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field, with a 22-yard pitch at the center. The objective for the batting team is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets, while the fielding team aims to prevent runs and dismiss batters. Cricket has various formats, including Test matches and Twenty20, and is governed by the International Cricket Council (ICC). The sport has a rich history dating back to the 16th century and is particularly popular in countries like India, Australia, and England.
